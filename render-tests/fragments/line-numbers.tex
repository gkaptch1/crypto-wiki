\textbf{Line numbers.}
Automatic numbering via the \emph{linenumbering} option:

\begin{center}
\procedure[linenumbering]{$\mathsf{Init}(\secpar)$}{
  k \sample \bin^\secpar \\
  T \gets \emptyset \\
  b \sample \bin \\
  \pcreturn b
}
\end{center}

Manual numbering of selected lines via \emph{pcln}:

\begin{center}
\procedure{$\mathsf{Manual}(x)$}{
  \pcln y \gets x + 7 \\
  z \gets y \cdot y \\
  \pcln \pcreturn z
}
\end{center}
