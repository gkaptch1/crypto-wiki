\textbf{Game hop.}
Two adjacent games in a hybrid argument; the change is highlighted:

\begin{pchstack}[center]
\procedure{$\mathsf{Game}_0$}{
  k \sample \bin^\secpar \\
  y \gets F(k, x) \\
  \pcreturn y
}
\pchspace
\procedure{$\mathsf{Game}_1$}{
  k \sample \bin^\secpar \\
  \gamechange{$y \sample \bin^n$} \\
  \pcreturn y
}
\end{pchstack}
